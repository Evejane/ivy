Hi, I'm Evejane a Web Developer

A web developer is a professional who specializes in the development of websites and web applications. They are responsible for designing, building, and maintaining websites that are user-friendly, visually appealing, and functional. Web developers typically have expertise in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, as well as experience with web development frameworks and content management systems (CMS). They work closely with designers, content creators, and other professionals to ensure that websites meet the needs of their intended audience, and may also be responsible for optimizing websites for search engines and ensuring that they are accessible to people with disabilities.
